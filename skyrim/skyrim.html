My Opinion

Skyrim is a action roll-play game developed and published by successful company bethesda. The predecessor to The
Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion skyrim has become the face of its genre. Its worldwide success cast it at the impressive place of 11
of the most sold games of all time, beating out games such as diablo III and grand theft auto V. It is a large open world game with
plenty of of main quests and even more side quests that could keep you busy for weeks. Although it may have a phonomonal
amount of quests all of them are as varied and interesting as the next. The NPCs in skyrim were some of the most groundbreaking
at its time of release, being able to marry npcs is a great part of the game, the same goes with the ability for the NPCs to change
their opinion of you depending on answers you give. The world of skyrim is stunning in different ways with its varied climates
throughout the map, from the silky white blankets of snow in the tundras to the green peaceful green forests every
part of the world has its own beauty.

Release Date: 11th November 2011

Developer: Bethesda Game Studios
