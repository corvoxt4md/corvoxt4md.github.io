My Opinion

Dishonored is easily my favourite game of all time even beating out skyrim. Although it lacks an open world and a beloved
rpg feel dishonored makes up for it with its ability to give the player a feeling as if the story is theirs, letting them hold the
reins and choose their path of either good or evil. The touching story of a bodyguard, falsely accused of murder he embarks on a
thrilling adventure to save his daughter, prove his innocence and bring justice to the real culprits. Featuring multiple endings
which are dependant on the way you play your way through the story. You will meet a diversity of characters each with their
own eloquently told backstories, leaving their ultimate fate in your hands, are they worthy of death? or will you turn the tides
and show mercy. And What results will this have on the ending and your treatment throughout the game?

Release Date: 9th October 2012

Developer: Arkane Studios

Publisher: Bethesda Softworks
